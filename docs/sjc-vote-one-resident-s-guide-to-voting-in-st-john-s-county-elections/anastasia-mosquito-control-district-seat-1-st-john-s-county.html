Anastasia Mosquito Control District Seat 1 (St John’s County)
Recommendation: Catherine Brandhorst Why: For the Anastasia Mosquito Control District (AMCD), my priorities are finding a responsible person who ideally has real experience/knowledge regarding mosquito control. I like that Catherine has significant experience working with pest control in her career, and was a business owner in this area. She seems very qualified to manage. I watched a good bit of the YouTube video of her below and she seems to be a good candidate who knows what she's talking about and is trying to manage things responsibly and not waste money. She also served as a Commissioner for AMCD previously.
